Biography

I am a security researcher in Bytedance Corporation now. Before that, I was a master student majoring in information security in Department of Electronic Engineering and Information Science, University of Science and Technology of China. I was supervised by Associate Professor Kaiping Xue in the Lab. of Information Network (InfoNet).

My reseach interests include the security of emerging networking, IoT security and cloud security. I've had the good fortune of working with some talented people Jie Xu, Huancheng Zhou.
